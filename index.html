FREE AND INFORMED CONSENT FORM (FICF)

1. Project Overview

You are invited to voluntarily participate in Projeto Raja, whose objective is to promote the identification, monitoring, and conservation of Brazilian rays through the integration of citizen science and scientific research.

2. Research Objective

This research seeks to collect information and photographic records that assist in species identification and in the analysis of occurrence patterns, distribution, and population trends of rays in Brazil.

3. Procedures

Participation consists of:

  • Completing a brief questionnaire;
  • Sharing voluntary photographs (e.g., fishing, diving, or observational records);
  • Providing data associated with the images, such as date, location, and relevant observations.

4. Data Use and Storage

The information and images provided will be used exclusively for scientific and academic purposes.

The data will remain under the responsibility of the Laborat√≥rio de Chondrichthyes (UFRJ), stored in a secure database for an indefinite period.

The data will not be commercialized or shared with third parties, except when required by law or by competent regulatory committees.

5. Confidentiality

Participants identities will be kept confidential. In publications, presentations, or scientific reports resulting from this research:

  • Participants will not be identified by name;
  • Data will be presented in aggregated and anonymized form;
  • Individual responses will not be highlighted.

6. Risks and Benefits

Any minimal risks are related to the voluntary disclosure of specimen data information, and measures will be adopted to ensure the protection and confidentiality of volunteers personal data.

There is no direct benefit to participants; however, your collaboration will contribute to the advancement of scientific knowledge and to the conservation of Brazilian rays.

7. Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may refuse to participate without any penalty or loss. There are no costs or financial compensation for participation.

8. Data Removal Policy

All participants have the right to request the deletion of their responses and/or images from the project database at any time, without the need for justification.

To do so, they must contact the project coordinators through the official channels provided in the questionnaire. Deletion will be carried out within a maximum of 24 hours after the request.
